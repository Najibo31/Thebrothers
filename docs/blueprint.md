# **App Name**: thebrothers

## Core Features:

- Smooth Scroll: Smooth scrolling navigation to different sections: Home, About Me, Media, Offers, and Contact.
- Dynamic Media Gallery: Dynamic photo and video gallery in the Media section with a call-to-action button to a dedicated Media page.
- Offer Presentation: Personal development offers section, each leading to a dedicated Offers page with a call-to-action and Baridi Mob payment link.
- Engaging Contact Section: Innovative contact section with a contact form, inspirational quote, social media icons, and an email contact.
- One-page layout: Displaying the content as a scrollable one-page layout, including: A home section, displaying the mission and objectives. The name Khotowet, motivation, transformation and personal growth are emphasized.
- About Me: An 'About me' section introduces the founder (Ouldali Yousra Nadjet), their background and values. There will also be a layout innovation, for example with a timeline, an image with hover texts, and quotes.
- Responsive Design: Responsive design ensuring optimal viewing experience across devices.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant orange (#E97451) to evoke feelings of energy, warmth, and enthusiasm, aligning with the motivational content.
- Background color: A desaturated dark blue (#22333B) provides a sophisticated, calming backdrop that helps the content stand out while maintaining a modern aesthetic.
- Accent color: A dark orange (#C75E3C), slightly less bright than the primary color, which helps draw attention to calls to action, while preserving readability.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif with an elegant feel, for headlines and short amounts of text.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif), will complement Playfair by being used for larger portions of body text. 'Playfair' is recommended only for headings.
- Clean and minimalist icons to represent different sections in the navigation and social media links in the contact and footer sections.
- Clean and elegant one-page scrollable layout with clear section divisions and engaging visuals to enhance user experience and motivation.
- Smooth scrolling animations when navigating between sections to provide a seamless and engaging user experience.
